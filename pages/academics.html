Our Academic Philosophy

At Bright Sun Sr. Sec. School, we focus on holistic development with special emphasis on social skills. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually while nurturing their creative, social, and emotional growth.

Affiliated with the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education, we provide comprehensive education from Playway to Class 12th. Our teaching methodology emphasizes conceptual understanding, critical thinking, and practical application of knowledge.

We offer specialized preparation for NEET/JEE and include Physical Education and NSS unit activities in our senior secondary program to ensure well-rounded development.

Examination & Assessment

Continuous Assessment

Regular evaluation through class tests, projects, and assignments to monitor student progress throughout the academic year.

  1. Monthly Unit Tests
  2. Project Work Assessment
  3. Class Participation Evaluation
  4. Homework & Assignment Checks
  5. Social Skills Assessment

Term Examinations

Formal examinations conducted twice a year to assess comprehensive understanding of the curriculum.

  1. Half-Yearly Examinations
  2. Annual Final Examinations
  3. Pre-Board Tests (Class 10 & 12)
  4. Practical Examinations

Board & Competitive Exams

Preparation and guidance for HP Board Examinations and competitive exams like NEET/JEE.

  1. Special Revision Classes
  2. Mock Board Examinations
  3. NEET/JEE Test Series
  4. Time Management Training
