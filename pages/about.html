About Bright Sun School

Illuminating Minds Since 1995 - A Legacy of Excellence in Education

Our Story

Established in 1995, Bright Sun Senior Secondary School has been a beacon of quality education in Barsar, Himachal Pradesh. From humble beginnings with just a handful of students, we have grown into a premier educational institution nurturing thousands of young minds.

Our journey of 25+ years is marked by consistent academic excellence, holistic development of students, and a commitment to providing affordable quality education to the children of Barsar and surrounding areas.

Affiliated with the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education, we offer education from Nursery to Class 12th, focusing on both academic rigor and character building.

Our Journey

1995

Foundation

Bright Sun School was established in Barsar with just 50 students and 5 teachers, starting with primary classes only.

2000

First Batch of Class 10

Our first batch of Class 10 students appeared for board exams with 100% pass rate, marking our entry into secondary education.

2005

Senior Secondary Expansion

School expanded to include Senior Secondary classes (10+2) with Science and Commerce streams, becoming a complete K-12 institution.

2012

First Merit Position

Our student secured 13th rank in HP Board Merit list (Class 10+2), establishing our reputation for academic excellence.

2018

Digital Transformation

Complete digital upgrade with smart classrooms, computer labs, and digital learning resources for all students.

2023

25 Years Celebration

Celebrated 25 years of educational excellence with 1000+ successful alumni and consistent top rankings in board results.
