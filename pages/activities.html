School Activities

Holistic Development Through Co-Curricular Activities

Our Activities Program

At Bright Sun Sr. Sec. School, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. Our diverse range of co-curricular activities ensures students develop physically, creatively, and socially while discovering their passions and talents.

Art & Craft

Creative expression through painting, pottery, sketching, and various craft techniques to nurture artistic talents.

Music & Dance

Training in classical and contemporary music and dance forms to develop rhythm, coordination, and performance skills.

Sports

Various indoor and outdoor sports activities including athletics, team games, and individual sports for physical fitness.

Yoga & Meditation

Regular yoga sessions and meditation practices to promote mental wellness, concentration, and stress management.

Karate & Self Defence

Martial arts training for self-defence, discipline, confidence building, and physical conditioning.

Debate & Elocution

Developing public speaking, argumentation, and communication skills through debates, speeches, and discussions.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

Community service activities that promote social awareness, responsibility, and leadership skills.
