About Bright Sun

Located in the beautiful Shivalik Hills of Himachal Pradesh, Bright Sun Sr. Sec. School offers a peaceful green campus surrounded by lush green trees and natural beauty.

Founded on 1st April, 1987 by the esteemed educationist Mr. R.K. Rana, the school is affiliated with the H.P. Board of School Education, Dharamshala. With 36+ years of excellence in academics & holistic development, Bright Sun has emerged as one of the leading schools in the region, devoted to nurturing values, knowledge and confident future.

Our campus offers students the opportunity to grow and learn in an inspiring environment. At Bright Sun School, Education has always been more than classroom learning - we encourage our students to explore, engage & experience learning in meaningful ways.
